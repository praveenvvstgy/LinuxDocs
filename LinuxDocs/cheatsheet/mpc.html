mpc

Music Player Client. Program for controlling the Music Player Daemon (MPD).

  • Toggle play/pause: 

mpc toggle

  • Stop playing: 

mpc stop

  • Show information about the currently playing song: 

mpc status

  • Play next song: 

mpc next

  • Play previous song: 

mpc prev

  • Forward or rewind the currently playing song: 

mpc [+-]{{seconds}}
