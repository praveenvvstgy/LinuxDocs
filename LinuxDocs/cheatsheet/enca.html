enca

Detect and convert encoding of text files.

  • Detect file(s) encoding according to your system's locale: 

enca {{file(s)}}

  • Detect file(s) encoding; -L option tells enca the current language; language is in the POSIX/C locale format, e.g. zh CN, en US etc: 

enca -L {{language}} {{file(s)}}

  • Convert file(s) to specified encoding: 

enca -L {{language}} -x {{to_encoding}} {{file(s)}}

  • Save original file as new file and convert new_file to specified encoding: 

enca -L {{language}} -x {{to_encoding}} < {{original_file}} > {{new_file}}
